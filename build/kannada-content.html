Lesson 0 · Kannada Basics

The Beginners Guide — script, sounds & how reading works

How Kannada works

Say what you see

Kannada is written the way it sounds. Read the letters left to right and you have said the word.

Capitals curl your tongue

A capital letter in our spellings (Ta, Da, La) means curl your tongue up and back — a fuller, hollow sound.

Doubled letters linger

A doubled consonant (appa, haLLi) is just held a beat longer than a single one.
