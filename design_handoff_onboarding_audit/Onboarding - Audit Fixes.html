Onboarding audit · findings

What's working

  • Strong brand throughout — chunky lips, Baloo titles, cream canvas, kolam texture.
  • Basics lesson pacing is excellent: one idea per screen, listen-first, honest copy.
  • Short intake (4 steps) with clear Back/Continue; lesson header + progress bar is clean.

Top issues (severity order)

  • 1 · Two competing progress systems. Dots at top say "screen 2 of 5" while the eyebrow says "Step 1 of 4" — off by one on every step. Keep exactly one: a 4-segment bar.
  • 2 · Name screen is half empty. Content is vertically centered, pushing the title below mid-screen; when the keyboard opens it jumps. Top-anchor all intake steps.
  • 3 · Red input border reads as an error. Red = action/validation in this system. Default input should be neutral (hairline + lip); red only on focus. The caret is also OS-blue — tint it red.
  • 4 · Personalization isn't skippable. Steps 2–3 gate progress behind disabled Continue. Add Skip — motivation and goal are nice-to-have, not required.
  • 5 · Step 2 hides options. Oversized cards push option 5+ below the fold with no scroll cue. Compact rows fit all six; checkbox affordance shows multi-select; "(0/3 selected)" becomes a counter that appears only after the first pick.
  • 6 · Step 3's (i) icons are dead weight. A tap-target inside a tap-target, and the payoff is hidden. Put it inline ("about one scenario") and preselect the recommended goal so Continue is never disabled here.
  • 7 · Off-palette green + grey. "You're ready" uses green checks (green is scoped to answer feedback only) and the quiz answer chips on 3/7 are flat grey (reads disabled). Both should be warm: gold reward circles; white chunky chips.
  • 8 · Vowel grid gives no feedback. "Tap each one" but tiles show no audio affordance and no heard/unheard state; red transliteration labels spend the one-red-moment 8 times. Add a speaker glyph, a gold "heard" state, a "3 of 8 heard" counter; labels go ink.
  • 9 · Welcome cards collide. The tilted value cards overlap each other and the bottom sheet clips card 3. Keep the playful tilt at ±1° with safe spacing.
Also recommended
  • 3/7, 6/7 and the post-intake greeting are now mocked below (row 4).
  • Name step — autofocus the field so the keyboard is already up.
  • Vowel tiles — require hearing all 8 before Next, or let Next pulse once the counter completes.
  • Splash — solid; consider the gold ಕ mark instead of ಬೇ on Welcome so splash → welcome feels continuous.
